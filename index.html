Emily Jena | WDD 130

My name is Emily Jena, from South Africa. I appreciate the nature that is usually around me when we travel. I like eating desserts for breakfast, but I am trying to change that habit. Whilst I was on my mission in Ghana, Accra, I learned how to make art with beads, and that is my current favorite thing to work on.

South Africa

South Africa really is many worlds in one. Expect stunning coastlines, dramatic mountainscapes, huge areas of untamed bushveld, starkly beautiful semi-deserts, lakes, waterfalls, spectacular canyons, forests and wide plains, plus Cape Town, undeniably one of the most beautiful cities in the world.
