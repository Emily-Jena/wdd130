Overview

Purpose

The Purpose of this 'Rafting Site Plan' is to have a set plan for having an emergency plan and safety management. where hazards are identified, exit points are clearly defined, emergency equipment can be located when needed. The plan should be operational and implemented for effective communication and regular complience.

Audience

The Rafting experience is for those that seek to have an adventure, maintaining the interests of various nature lover, thrill seeker, fitness enthusiasts, family fun. Of all ages and size.
